Introduction to Nutrition

Nutrition is essential for maintaining overall health, supporting growth, and ensuring the proper functioning of your body. A well-balanced diet provides the necessary nutrients, including macronutrients and micronutrients, to keep you energized and healthy.

Carbohydrates

Your body's primary energy source, found in bread, pasta, rice, and fruits. Carbohydrates break down into glucose to fuel muscles and brain function.

Proteins

Essential for building and repairing tissues, producing enzymes and hormones. Found in meats, fish, eggs, and legumes.

Fats

Crucial for energy storage, cell structure, and vitamin absorption. Healthy fats from avocados, nuts, and olive oil support heart health.

Micronutrients Matter

Vitamins and minerals play vital roles in maintaining body functions, supporting immunity, and promoting growth. A balanced diet incorporating fiber aids digestion and helps regulate blood sugar levels.

Balanced Nutrition

Achieving the right balance of these nutrients is key to staying fit, active, and healthy. Proper nutrition also plays an important role in weight management, muscle gain, and overall wellness.
