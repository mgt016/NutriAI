Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a meal plan?

To create a meal plan, go to the "My Diet" page and click on the "Create Meal Plan" button. Follow the prompts to select your dietary preferences and nutritional goals.

How can I track my calorie intake?

You can track your calorie intake by logging your meals in the "My Diet" page. The system will automatically calculate and display your daily calorie consumption.

How do I update my profile information?

To update your profile information, go to the "Profile" page and click the "Edit Profile" button. You can edit your personal details there and then click the "Save Changes" button.
